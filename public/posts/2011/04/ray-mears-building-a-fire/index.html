Ray Mears Building a Fire

April 17, 2011

Ray Mears is an expert woodsman from southern Great Britan. I remember watching him on a U.S. cable network a few years ago. They can still be found on UKTV but I can’t find them in the U.S. Thankfully there are a number of clips on line like the one embedded above. Mears is a serious student and teacher of bushcraft not a sensationalist unlike another British import whose show is (unfortunately) much more popular on U.S. televisions. He has authored many books available (with a selection of gear and related items at his website .
